Philosophy

I believe technology should serve as a mirror to our minds—not a shortcut. In both education and AI, I value tools that prompt deeper thinking, promote epistemic diversity, and restore agency to learners.

  • Educational communities are the most sustainable path to transformation—they build voice, capacity, and self-trust.
  • Product managers must advocate for thoughtful iteration—especially in domains that affect cognition, safety, or access.
  • AI should be used to foster critical thinking, not replace it. I live by a personal AI use policy rooted in learning and authenticity.
